Some Cool Facts About Me

My Studies

I began my journey into web development by exploring various free tutorials on YouTube. Seeking to deepen my knowledge, I moved on to using W3Schools, which provided a solid theoretical foundation. Later, I decided to invest in courses on Codecademy, not only to learn in a more structured way but also to obtain recognized certifications. This combination of self-study and certified courses allowed me to develop strong skills and gain official recognition in the field of web development.

My Experience

My web development experience is rooted in a strong passion for creating websites. I started by asking friends and acquaintances to describe their ideal websites, including their tastes and needs. I would then design and build these sites, striving to meet their expectations and preferences. This hands-on approach provided me with valuable insights into user requirements and helped me refine my skills in creating customized web solutions. Over time, I developed a deeper understanding of web design principles and user experience, continually improving my ability to deliver websites that truly catered to individual needs and desires.

My Creativity

Creativity has always been an integral part of who I am. From drawing and painting to writing and music, I have continuously explored various forms of artistic expression. These experiences have collectively contributed to my refined creative taste. Each discipline has taught me different aspects of creativity, which I now apply to my web development projects, ensuring they are both visually appealing and engaging.
